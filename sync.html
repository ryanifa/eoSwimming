How it works

Your swim data starts at t = 0 (the moment the eo paddles started recording). Your video starts at its own t = 0. The offset is how many seconds into the video the swim data should begin: if your swim starts at 5.20 s into the video, set swim_start_seconds = 5.20.

Once you've found the value, run the Composite video with overlay workflow and paste this number.
